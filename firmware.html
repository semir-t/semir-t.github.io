About me

I have experience developing firmware from low level drivers to system application on MCUs and SOCs from varius manufacturers:

  • STMicroelectronics
  • Microchip
  • Atmel
  • Nordic
  • NXP
  • Espressif
  • GigaDevice

In last couple of years I have worked with various MCUs and some of them have following characteristics:

  • Based on the ARM Cortex M0,M1,M3,M4,M7 processor
  • 8 ,16 and 32 bit MCUs
  • Dual core MCU (STM32H7x7, STM32H7x5)

I have in depth knowledge of most common MCU peripherals such as UART, SPI, I2C, CAN, DMA, ADC,DAC, RTC, Timers, USB, CAN, LTDC, FMC, etc.

As new ICs are coming to the market there is always a need to write custom drivers, and in the last couple of years I gain experience in writing drivers and libraries for

  • Varius sensors (temperature,accelerometers, pressure etc.)
  • Motor drivers (stepper motors, BLDC, DC, Servo)
  • LCD,TFT and OLED displays with dedicated Capacitive or Resistive touch interface
  • MMC/SD card driver
  • GSM modules
  • GPRS modules
  • WiFi modules
  • BLE modules

Depending on the requirements, I can also implemented:

  • Secure Bootloader
  • OTA firmware upgrade
  • Self-testing algorithm
  • GUI interface
